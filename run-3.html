Welcome to Run 3!

Embark on a thrilling adventure as you run and jump through space tunnels in Run 3! Explore the galaxy, unlock new characters, and take on endless challenges.

How to Play

Ready for a galactic challenge? Here's how to navigate the space tunnels:

  1. Use the Arrow Keys: Press the left and right arrow keys to move your character across the tunnels. Jump over gaps with the Spacebar or Up Arrow key.
  2. Switch Walls: Move left or right enough to land on the walls of the tunnels and keep your run going!
  3. Explore Mode: Complete levels and explore the galaxy, unlocking new tunnels and characters.
  4. Infinite Mode: Challenge yourself with endless tunnels. Collect power cells to unlock new characters but be careful not to fall out of the tunnel—game over if you do!

Run 3 TIPS

Here are some expert tips to help you conquer the tunnels:

  • New Aliens, New Tricks: Unlock 9 characters, each with unique abilities. Use power cells to unlock characters faster and adapt to different challenges.
  • Control Your Jumps: Hold the up arrow to jump higher or tap it for a smaller jump to avoid overshooting platforms.
  • Look Ahead: Keep your focus on the upcoming platforms. Rotate the tunnels and plan your jumps carefully to avoid falling off.
  • Infinite Practice: Use Infinite Mode to collect power cells and practice your skills in a variety of tunnels.

Run 3 Questions

Here are a few frequently asked questions about Run 3:

  • Can you still play Run 3 without Flash? Yes, Run 3 is now playable without Flash. It's been converted to HTML to ensure continued access after Adobe Flash Player was discontinued.
  • How old is Run 3? Run 3 was released between 2013 and 2014, and became a fan favorite after it was uploaded to Coolmath Games in 2017.
  • Is the Run 3 character a girl? Yes, the first character, Runner, is female. As you progress, you can unlock many different characters with unique abilities, including Skater, Lizard, and Bunny!
