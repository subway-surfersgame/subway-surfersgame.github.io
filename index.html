Introduction of Hills of Steel

Hills of Steel is an exciting action-packed online game. Take your tank through challenging terrains and dominate online battles, and become a steel champion.

Experience the adrenaline rush of piloting powerful tanks, outmaneuvering opponents, and emerging victorious across diverse battlefields.

Hills Of Steel Gameplay

Hills of Steel offers a perfect blend of action and strategy. You'll control a tank, navigating through hilly terrains while blasting enemy vehicles into oblivion. The physics-based gameplay adds a layer of realism, making every shot and collision feel impactful. As you progress, you'll collect loot, upgrade your tank, and unlock new ones, each with unique strengths and play styles.

The Features

  • Explosive Online Battles: Engage in real-time PvP battles against players worldwide.
  • Tank Customization: Unleash your inner engineer! Customize your tank with a variety of upgrades and weapons to fit your combat strategy.
  • Multiple Game Modes: Test your skills in various modes like Adventure, where you collect loot and conquer challenging levels, or hone your reflexes in the fast-paced Survival mode.
  • Regular Events: Participate in weekly challenges to earn exclusive rewards and climb the leaderboards to prove your dominance.
  • Clan Warfare: Team up with friends and fellow tankers to form a clan and conquer the online battlefield together.

Tips to Play Hills Of Steel

  • Master the Terrain: Use the hills and obstacles to your advantage. Take cover, outmaneuver your opponents, and unleash surprise attacks.
  • Know Your Tank: Every tank has unique strengths and weaknesses. Experiment with different tanks and find one that suits your playstyle.
  • Upgrade Strategically: Don't spread your upgrades thin. Focus on a specific playstyle and upgrade components that enhance your tank's strengths.
  • Utilize Power-Ups: Wisely use power-ups like mines, force fields, and airstrikes to gain a tactical edge in battle.

Controls

  • Move: use the A and D keys or the Arrow keys to drive your tank!
  • Shoot: use the spacebar to shoot!
  • Repair: press the B button to repair your tank
