Temple Run 2: The Ultimate Endless Adventure

Join the fearless treasure hunter as he escapes with the gold idol, racing against time and avoiding obstacles while being chased by evil monkeys!

How to Play Temple Run 2

Keyboard Controls:

  • Move: Use AD or Left/Right arrow keys
  • Jump: Use W or Up arrow key
  • Slide Down: Use S or Down arrow key

Mobile Controls:

  • Move and Turn: Swipe left or right
  • Jump: Swipe up
  • Slide: Swipe down

Game Features

  • Endless Running: The game never ends! Keep running until you collide with an obstacle or get overtaken by the demon monkeys.
  • Various Environments: Run through cliffs, forests, mines, and more!
  • Power Meter: Collect coins to fill your power meter and unlock temporary speed and invincibility boosts!
  • Monster Chase: Evil Demon Monkeys chase you, and you must avoid obstacles while escaping!

About the Game

Temple Run 2 is the second installment of the legendary Temple Run series, developed by Imangi Studios. It offers an exciting endless running experience where players must escape from enemy monsters while collecting coins, upgrading abilities, and avoiding a series of traps and obstacles.

Game Modes

The game has no definitive end; instead, it continues until the player collides with a large obstacle, falls into the water, or is caught by the demon monkeys. Your goal is to survive as long as possible while collecting coins and completing objectives.

Power-Ups and Abilities

  • Boosts: Unlock boosts like Shield Duration, Coin Magnet, and Boost Distance to improve your running experience.
  • Abilities: Upgrade your character's stats for better performance in the game.

Does Temple Run 2 end?

No! As an endless running game, Temple Run 2 keeps going until your character collides with an obstacle, falls into water, or gets overtaken by the chasing monkeys.

What is the Power Meter?

The Power Meter fills up as you collect coins. Once it's full, you'll receive a temporary speed and invincibility boost!
