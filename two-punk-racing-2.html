How to Play

Player 1 Controls:

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • Nitro: M
  • Look Back: B

Player 2 Controls:

  • Move: W, A, S, D
  • Nitro: Y
  • Look Back: C

Other Controls:

  • Level Restart: R

Game Tips:

  • Use speeder signs on the road to accelerate your car!
  • When jumping off ramps, keep your car balanced by moving left or right to stay on track.
  • Maintain high speed on curvy roads to minimize the effects of gravity and keep control of your vehicle!
