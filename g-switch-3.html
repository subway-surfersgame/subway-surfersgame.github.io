Welcome to G-Switch 3: Defy Gravity and Master Your Surroundings!

Ready for a gravity-defying adventure? G-Switch 3 challenges you to switch between walls, floors, and ceilings while navigating deadly traps. Play solo in Campaign and Endless modes or battle with friends in Multiplayer mode!

Game Controls

G-Switch 3 offers simple but effective controls to help you conquer the gravity-defying challenges:

  • Left Mouse Button / Any Keyboard Key: Press to switch between walls, floors, and ceilings. Master the timing to survive the traps and obstacles!

How to Play G-Switch 3

In G-Switch 3, your goal is to navigate the deadly gauntlet of traps by switching gravity at the right moment. Here's how you can dive into the action:

  1. Start the game in one of the available modes: Multiplayer, Campaign, or Endless.
  2. Switch between walls, floors, and ceilings by pressing the left mouse button or any key on your keyboard.
  3. Time your switches to avoid obstacles and survive longer in the gauntlet.
  4. In Multiplayer mode, play with up to 7 friends or challenge AI opponents.
  5. Compete to reach the highest score and master the deadly environment!

Tips and Tricks for Success

To master G-Switch 3 and become the ultimate gravity-defying champion, consider these helpful strategies:

  • Play Multiplayer: Test your skills against real players or AI opponents in the fun Multiplayer mode. Compete with friends or team up to survive the traps.
  • Timing is Everything: Switch gravity just before you encounter an obstacle instead of waiting to hit it. This helps avoid collisions and gives you more control.
  • Find Your Preferred Controls: You can use either the mouse or the keyboard to play the game. Experiment to see what feels most comfortable for you!
  • Focus on Precision: The key to survival in G-Switch 3 is precise timing. Stay calm and plan your moves carefully as you navigate the constantly changing gravity.

About the Creator

G-Switch 3 is developed by Serius Games, a game development company based in Portugal. The company is known for creating the G-Switch series, including the original G-Switch and G-Switch 2. Serius Games continues to innovate in the genre of fast-paced, gravity-defying skill games.
