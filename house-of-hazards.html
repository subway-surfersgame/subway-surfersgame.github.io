Welcome to House of Hazards!

Can you finish your housework while avoiding the many dangers around your house? Play now to compete with friends in this fun and challenging game!

Game Description

House of Hazards is a competitive skill game where players have to complete various housework tasks while avoiding dangerous obstacles. Play with up to 4 players, complete missions, and be careful not to get hit by hazards lurking around your house. Complete tasks faster and outwit your rivals to win!

Gameplay

In House of Hazards, you will be assigned multiple tasks that you must complete while avoiding obstacles. The goal is to finish the tasks faster than your opponents, but beware—the house is filled with unexpected dangers!

  • Drink Coffee: Start your day with a cup of coffee by heading to the kitchen.
  • Brush Teeth: Run to the bathroom and brush your teeth.
  • Pick up the Watering Can: Water your garden by picking up the watering can from the garden.
  • Grab Mail: Head to the garage and outside to grab the mail, then return safely.

After completing the tasks, the scoreboard will display your progress, showing the completion rate of all players. Higher completion rates will give you an advantage during the next round!

Hazards to Avoid

Throughout the house, you’ll face various dangers that can stop you from completing tasks:

  • Ceiling Light: It can fall on your head at any moment, causing you to faint.
  • The Cupboard: Beware of doors suddenly opening. Crawl to avoid crashing into them.
  • The Faucet: The faucet may randomly turn on and spray water. Run fast to avoid it!
  • The Toaster Oven: Bread will jump out and hit you. Be ready to dodge!
  • The Robot Vacuum: Avoid the vacuum by jumping over it as it moves.
  • The Remote and Mirror: The remote controller can fire at you through the mirror. Be cautious!

In the garden, you'll face teddy bears that chase you, swings that automatically swing, and water hazards from the tussock. In the garage, watch out for pitching machines, skateboards, and falling water pipes!

Game Modes

Choose from two exciting game modes:

  • Multiplayer Mode: Compete with up to 3 other players to complete tasks and sabotage each other. Use the down arrow key to create dangers for your rivals or lock them in a chest!
  • Time Trial Mode: Complete all the tasks on your own as fast as possible and see how quickly you can finish!

Controls

Use these controls to play the game:

PLAYER 1:

  • Move: A (left), D (right)
  • Fire or Crouch: S
  • Jump: W

PLAYER 2:

  • Move: J (left), L (right)
  • Fire or Crouch: K
  • Jump: Y

Note: The game allows up to 4 players, with two players using the keyboard and others using a gamepad.

Unlock Characters

There are 8 characters in the game, but only half of them are available at the start. Unlock new characters like Robo Rob, Rocking Grandma, Super Sam, and Dom by breaking records in Multiplayer Mode!

About the Game

House of Hazards was created by New Eich Games and released in September 2020. The game has received updates to improve gameplay and attract a global audience. It’s playable on both PC and mobile web browsers as an HTML game, allowing you to enjoy the action anytime, anywhere!
